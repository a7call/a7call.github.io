Game Description

You are two components of Unity. Careless developers make you rebel, you decide to run away. Your escape causes great damage to the software, however you continue your adventure until the final level. Your only way out: to crash Unity.

Intentions

Break Unity is a humorous game aimed at an audience used to shoot them up, but not necessarily hardcore gamers: it is aimed at more casual gamers as well. It obviously has a tendency to appeal to people accustomed to using the Unity editor through a large number of Easter Eggs. The main target of the game is therefore first and foremost Unity users. The game is also intended to be dynamic and responsive, so that the dexterity challenge it offers is stimulating and challenging. Finally, Break Unity offers puzzles to solve despite the pressure imposed by the enemies.
