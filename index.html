Space Wander

Project description

  • Collaborative project
  • Team size: 4
  • My roles: Team Lead, Creative director, Lead Programmer
  • Engine: Unity2D
  • Mode: Single Player
  • TimeFrame: 6 months+
  • Status: In Development
  • Release: To be determined
  • GDD (One pager): Link to GDD

Game description

As a group of four, the project "Space Wander" has been in development since June 2020. I have always had the desire to create a game on a larger, challenging scale from than my previous projects. Space Wander is a Roguelike set in an apocalyptic future where monsters from outer space are at the gates of planet earth. You play as a combat-trained astronaut whose goal is to defeat alien forces in order to save humanity. You will then be brought to fight the invaders with powerful and original weapons in a transcendent atmosphere, to finish in the depths of Tartarus where you will discover the dark intentions which motivate your enemies to invade the Earth. Its dynamism, its innovative combat system and its atypical atmosphere constitute the pillars of its design. The target is a player of at least 14 years old that enjoy a fast-paced fighting / shooter game. They should always persevere and never be afraid to take on a challenge.

Goals and Design Intentions

The goal is to make the game marketable, while developing skills like character, enemy, and combat design, procedural programming and management and organization of long-term projects. The design intentions of Space Wander are to ensure a dynamic game with an innovative combat system. The objective is to allow the player to experience a storyline in which they are truly the protagonist. Any player must feel a sense of omnipotence and test their knowledge. The key to a successful game is to ensure an inspiring experience in which players can learn and expand their skills.

My Contribution

Being at the forefront of the project, I lead and coordinate the tasks of the team. In addition to piloting the project as a whole, I am responsible for the technical programming and the executive creative directions. I also currently contribute to the graphic art.

Conclusion

This project allows me to strengthen on the one hand my core business and technical skills in design and programming, and on the other hand my managerial and organizational skills. Working with four other people allows me to improve my teamwork. Today I listen to everyone’s issues, I also try to coordinate individual efforts in order to get the best out of our collaboration. It is essential that each member finds their place and feels comfortable in the team. The next step is to work with a graphic designer to achieve a visual that perfectly matches the design intentions. We want to release a first version testable by a sample of players in early 2022.

Shape Dungeon

Project description

  • Solo project
  • Engine: Unity3D
  • Mode: Single Player
  • TimeFrame: 2 weeks
  • Status: Released
  • GDD (One pager): Link to GDD

Game description

Shape Dungeon is my first attempt at Puzzle Game. In this game you play as a small being whose ability is to change shape. Your goal is to get out of the terrifying dungeon you are locked in. To do this you will have to face and solve the puzzles of the dungeon. The target is a player of all ages looking to relax, he is a puzzle enthusiast. He likes fluid and relaxing experiences.

Goals and Design Intentions

The objective is to experience the creation of a puzzle game. This game allows me to develop my puzzle design skills. Also the development of this game allows me to approach video game design in a different way. In this precise case, the development of a positive, calm and restful universe. The design intentions are to create a satisfying experience that brings a bit of challenge. The experience should be fluid, pleasant and rewarding.

My Contribution

I made this game on my own, however the assets and music are from the Unity asset store.

Conclusion

The next step would be to create new levels, develop new puzzle mechanics and environments to keep the players' interest.

BreakUnity

Project description

  • Collaborative project
  • Team size: 3
  • Game Jam
  • Engine: Unity3D
  • Mode: Co-op
  • TimeFrame: 24h
  • Status: Released

Game description

In BreakUnity you embodie two Unity components. Careless developers make you rebel, you decide to run away. Your escape causes great damage to the software, however you continue your adventure until the final level. Your only way out: to crash Unity.

Design Intentions

Break Unity is a humorous game aimed at an audience of casual gamers used to shoot them up. It obviously has a tendency to appeal to people accustomed to using the Unity editor through a large number of Easter Eggs. The main target of the game is therefore first and foremost Unity users. The game is also intended to be dynamic and responsive, so that the dexterity challenge it offers is stimulating. Finally, Break Unity offers puzzles to solve in addition to the pressure imposed by the enemies.

My Contribution

I codesigned the game and did all of the programming. This was a very challenging experience, we wanted to finish the game is less than 24 hours, which we achieved!

Prison Break

Project description

  • Collaborative project
  • Team size: 3
  • Game Jam
  • Engine: Unity2D
  • Mode: Co-op
  • TimeFrame: 48h
  • Status: Released

Game description

Prison Break is a game made in 48 hours as part of the Game jam GMTK which took place during the weekend of June 11, 2021. With two friends, we decided to participate in this edition of GMTK Game jam. This year the theme was: “Joined together”. For the design of this game, we opted for an action game. Michael and Lincoln escaped from prison. Tied by the hands the two prisoners must flee, the rope which hinders them allows them to defeat the public forces. Watch out for the obstacles on your way, they could make you lose your head!

Design Intentions

For this game we wanted to create a simple and fun experience, we also wanted to focus on the “polish” aspect of the experience in order to come up with the best possible version.

My Contribution

I did most of the programming, including the simulation of rope physics. This functionality seemed essential to us for the immersion we wanted to offer. At the same time, I did the vast majority of visual art, as well as co-designed the levels.

Conclusion

By working with the same team as for the previous Game jam we were able to further strengthen our ability to work as a team and we have improved our organization. Personally, I improved my programming skills on the Unity game engine by discovering and experimenting with different ways to simulate physics on things like rope or chaines.

Danse Is Cast

Project description

  • Collaborative project
  • Team size: 3
  • Game Jam
  • Engine: Unity2D
  • Mode: Co-op
  • TimeFrame: 48h
  • Status: Released

Game description

Danse Is Cast is a game produced in 48 hours as part of the Game Jam TUTOFR2 which took place during the weekend of May 1, 2021. With two friends we decided to participate in this edition of Game Jam TUTOFR2. This year the theme was: “Alea jacta est” (the die is cast). For the design of this game, we opted for a musical game on the theme of disco. In Dance Is Cast, you play as a prodigious dancer named Michael whose dream is to become the king of disco music. You’ll make your way through the dance floors, each one more inflamed than the last, to reach the “Disco Throne”. Some slabs have been trapped, you will need to use your memory and dance skills to foil the traps in order to achieve your goal as King of Pop. The dance is cast!

My Contribution

During this jam, we jointly designed the game. In addition I programmed most of the expected features. For example, I personally created the “tempo” system. This system makes it possible to force the player to move to the beat of the music. If the player is out of beat, he is eliminated. I also did most of the visual art and also co-designed the levels.

Conclusion

This project allowed me to strengthen my teamwork skills. I also improved my technical skills so that I could get the game out on time. We learned to compromise on certain elements in order to be able to release a game of the quality we wanted.

FisherMan

Project description

  • Solo project
  • Engine: Unity2D
  • Mode: Single Player
  • TimeFrame: 2 months+
  • Status: Released
  • Platforme: Playstore

Game description

The FisherMan is a game developed for the mobile platform during the spring of 2020. The concept is simple, you play as a fisher / sushi master whose goal is to transform as many fish as possible into delicious sushi. In your quest for fresh fish, you will need to beware of any surprises the ocean may hide.

My Contribution

The FisherMan is my first commercial video game project. After a few months of exploring and learning the skills necessary to create a game, I decided to embark on an original project. The visual art, sound effects, and design and development were all done by me.

Conclusion

This project allowed me to put into practice the lessons I had acquired during the previous months. I improved not only in my chosen field of programming, but also in design and art (visual, sound). As a result of this project, I was better able to understand the different needs of the video game professions. I was also able to confirm my passion for video games and their creation.
