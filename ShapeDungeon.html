Game Description

Shape Dungeon is my first attempt at Puzzle Game. In this game you play as a small being whose ability is to change shape. Your goal is to get out of the terrifying dungeon you are locked in. To do this you will have to face and solve the puzzles of the dungeon.

The target is a player of all ages looking to relax, he is a puzzle enthusiast. He likes fluid and relaxing experiences.

Goals and Design Intentions

The objective is to experience the creation of a puzzle game. This game allows me to develop my puzzle design skills. Also the development of this game allows me to approach video game design in a different way. In this precise case, the development of a positive, calm and restful universe.

The design intentions are to create a satisfying experience that brings a bit of challenge. The experience should be fluid, pleasant and rewarding.

My Contribution

I made this game on my own, however the assets and music are from the asset store.

Conclusion

The next step would be to create new levels, develop new puzzle mechanics and environments to keep the player's interest.
