Game Description

Prison Break is a game made in 48 hours as part of the Game jam GMTK which took place during the weekend of June 11, 2021. With two friends, we decided to participate in this edition of GMTK Game jam. This year the theme was: “Joined together”. For the design of this game, we opted for an action game. Michael and Lincoln escaped from prison. Tied by the hands the two prisoners must flee, the rope which hinders them allows them to defeat the public forces. Watch out for the obstacles on your way, they could make you lose your head!

Intentions

For this game we wanted to create a simple and fun experience, we also wanted to focus on the “polish” aspect of the experience in order to come up with the best possible version.

My Contribution

I did most of the programming, including the simulation of rope physics. This functionality seemed essential to us for the immersion we wanted to offer. At the same time, I did the vast majority of visual art, as well as co-designed the levels.

Conclusion

By working with the same team as for the previous Game jam we were able to further strengthen our ability to work as a team and we have improved our organization. Personally, I improved my programming skills on the Unity game engine by discovering and experimenting with different ways to simulate physics on things like rope or chaines.
