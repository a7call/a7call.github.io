Game Description

The FisherMan is a game developed for the mobile platform during the spring of 2020. The concept is simple, you play as a fisher / sushi master whose goal is to transform as many fish as possible into delicious sushi. In your quest for fresh fish, you will need to beware of any surprises the ocean may hide.

My Contribution

The FisherMan is my first commercial video game project. After a few months of exploring and learning the skills necessary to create a game, I decided to embark on an original project. The visual art, sound effects, and design and development were all done by me.

Conclusion

This project allowed me to put into practice the lessons I had acquired during the previous months. I improved not only in my chosen field of programming, but also in design and art (visual, sound). As a result of this project, I was better able to understand the different needs of the video game professions. I was also able to confirm my passion for video games and their creation.
