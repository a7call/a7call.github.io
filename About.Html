About me

Hi! I'm Valentin, I am from Paris. I recently graduated as an engineer from Insa Lyon, I am now studying game design at Gobelins in Paris in the Interactive Digital Experience master. I am currently looking for opportunities in the gaming industry. I have been fascinated by video games for almost my entire life For the last two years I have experienced a diverse range of situations that shaped me to become the designer I am today. I like to show my passion for video games and attention to detail on the projects I work on. I like to think before I act. I put careful thought in my design decisions, and focus on what is feasible and ideal for the projects I work on. My technical background as an engineer allow me to be rigorous and committed in each of my initiatives
