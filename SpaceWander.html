Game Description

Accompanied by a team of 4 people, I have been developing Space Wander since June 2020. This project was born out of a desire to make a game on a larger scale than my previous projects. Space Wander is a Roguelike set in an apocalyptic future where aliens / demons are at the gates of planet earth. You play as a combat-trained astronaut whose goal is to defeat alien forces in order to save humanity. You will then be brought to fight the invaders with powerful and original weapons in a transcendent atmosphere, to finish in the depths of Tartarus where you will discover the dark intentions which motivate your enemies to invade the Earth. Its dynamism, its innovative combat system and its atypical atmosphere constitute the pillars of its design.

The target is a player of at least 14 years old who enjoys a fast-paced fighting / shooter game. He is not afraid of the challenge.

Goals and Design Intentions

The goal is to make the game marketable, while developing skills like character, enemy, and combat design, procedural programming and management and organization of long-term projects.

the design intentions of spacewander are to create a dynamic game with an innovative combat system. The objective is to allow the player to have an experience in which he is truly the protagonist. I as a player must feel a sense of omnipotence. A key to successful design is the exposure that the experience provides.

My Contribution

Being at the initiative of the project, I lead and coordinate the activities of the team. In addition to piloting the project as a whole, I am responsible for programming and creative directions. Also I did most of the current art.
